To Play:
Click the right-hand-side deck and move your piece to the corresponding color. Single color cards will allow you reach the Kandy Kastle. You must move your piece to the picture squares if you turn over a corresponding character card. Keep moving until you reach the Kandy Kastle (rainbow card)! If the deck returns the rainbow card, you've won!

Plumpy Character Card = plum square
Mr. Mint Character Card = candy cane square
Jolly Character Card = gumdrop square
Gramma Nutt Character Card = peanut square
Princess Lolly Character Card = lollipop square
Queen Frostine Character Card = snowflake square
